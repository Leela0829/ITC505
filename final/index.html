Addendum

  • The creation of the "Revenge of Lights Out" game was an enlightening journey, merging imagination with problem-solving.
  • The primary aim was to craft a dynamic and captivating game that challenges participants.

Innovative Choices

  • Game Design: Opting for a 5x5 grid struck a harmonious balance between complexity and accessibility for players with varied skill levels.
  • User Interface: Embracing a minimalist approach prioritized gameplay, aligning with contemporary design principles emphasizing simplicity and functionality.
  • Feedback Mechanism: Implementing a modal window for announcing game completion aimed to deliver clear and gratifying feedback to players.

Hurdles and Resolutions

  • Randomizing the Board: Initial difficulty arose in effectively shuffling the board at the game's onset.
    This was tackled by introducing a randomization function to ensure a fresh experience in every session.
  • Ensuring Playability: Guaranteeing the game's solvability after shuffling posed a challenge. This was overcome through meticulous testing and refinement of the algorithm.

Noteworthy Observations

  • User-Centric Design: The significance of prioritizing user experience became evident. Streamlined gameplay and interface design profoundly enhance engagement.
  • Testing is Essential: Thorough testing was indispensable in identifying and resolving issues, highlighting the critical role of comprehensive testing in game development.
